Lasagna Recipe

Description

This lasagna recipe is made with a meaty, made from scratch tomato sauce and a deliciously cheese filling. Traditionally homeade lasagna would be made with ricotta but this calls for a blend of small curd cottage cheese and Parmesan.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
