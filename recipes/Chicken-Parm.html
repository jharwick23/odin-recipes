Chicken Parmesan Recipe

Description

Chicken parmesan made with a cheese spread using ricotta fortified with sharp cheddar

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Salt and pepper
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Bread crumbs
  • Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • Ricotta cheese and cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Pound chicken breast, season with salt and pepper, spring with flour on each side
  3. Pour eggs over chicken and cover with bread crumbs
  4. Cook chicken in skillet and mix cheese in bowl
  5. Bake the cheese and chicken
